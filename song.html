Songs

On this site you can manage all your songs. On the left, you can upload new songs to the server. And on the right side is an overview about all tunes which have been already uploaded.
Important note: Only mp3-files are allowed. Furthermore, each song needs at minimum the following id3-tags: title, artist and genre. Otherwise, the server won't be able to assign songs correctly and to play-back them.
